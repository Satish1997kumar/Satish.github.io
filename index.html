Project

Role: Team Leader

Title: Fabrication of ZA-27 Hybrid Metal Matrix Composite by Stir Casting

Description: The main aim of this project is to fabricate low cost, self-lubricating bearing material by reinforcing Zinc Aluminium alloy with hard filler materials. These alloys with low cost, low energy requirement for shaping, excellent cast ability, and high strength properties are equivalent or better than some standard bronze bearing alloys, but they still have limited application especially due to the deterioration of mechanical and wear resistance properties at temperatures exceeding 100°C. One promising approach to improve the elevated temperature properties is by reinforcing the alloys with ceramic particles (micro sized) as suggested by various hypothesis. Hence in this work an effort has been made to fabricate ZA-27 Hybrid metal matrix composite by stir casting by adding ZA-27+Zirconsand+solid lubricant’s like graphite , moly etc.
